Motion exists to tell the festival story.

Cards gently scale and elevate when active.

Soft god rays.
Lamp flame flicker.
Banana leaf sway.
Fabric movement.
Occasional birds.

No sudden camera movement.

8. Animation Timing
9. Performance Budgets
